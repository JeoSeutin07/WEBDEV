H.O.T (High-five of Teenagers)

H.O.T (High-five of Teenagers): H.O.T was a South Korean boy band formed in 1996 by SM Entertainment. They are known as the "High-five of Teenagers" and are considered one of the first Kpop idol groups. H.O.T achieved massive popularity in the late 1990s with hits like "Candy" and "We Are The Future".
