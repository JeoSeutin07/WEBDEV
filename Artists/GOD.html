g.o.d

g.o.d (Groove Over Dose): g.o.d is a South Korean boy band formed in 1999 by Sidus HQ. They are known for their soulful ballads and catchy pop songs, which earned them a large following. g.o.d achieved great success in the early 2000s with hits like "To Mother" and "One Candle".
