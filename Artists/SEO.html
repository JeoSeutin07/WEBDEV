Seo Taiji and Boys

Seo Taiji and Boys were a South Korean music group formed in 1992 by Seo Taiji. They are considered pioneers of modern Kpop and are credited with revolutionizing the Korean music industry. Seo Taiji and Boys' music incorporated various genres like pop, rap, and rock, and their performances were known for their unique style.
