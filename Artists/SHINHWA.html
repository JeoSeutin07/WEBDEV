Shinhwa

Shinhwa is a South Korean boy band formed in 1998 by SM Entertainment. They are one of the longest-running Kpop groups and are known for their impressive vocal and dance abilities. Shinhwa has released numerous albums and is considered a legendary group in the Kpop industry.
