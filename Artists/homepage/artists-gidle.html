About (G)I-DLE


(G)I-DLE is a South Korean girl group formed by Cube Entertainment in 2018. The group consists of six members: Miyeon, Minnie, Soojin, Soyeon, Yuqi, and Shuhua. The group's name, (G)I-DLE, is a combination of the word "girl" and the phrase "I-DLE," which represents the members' determination to never become idle in their music career.

(G)I-DLE debuted with their single "LATATA" in 2018, which received positive reviews and charted well on the music charts. Since then, they have released several hit songs such as "HANN (Alone)," "Senorita," and "Oh My God." The group is known for their unique and powerful music style, as well as their stunning visuals and strong stage presence. Soyeon, the group's leader, is also known for her songwriting and producing skills, having composed and produced many of their songs.

(G)I-DLE has received numerous awards and nominations since their debut, including several "Rookie of the Year" awards and "Best Female Group" awards. They have also gained a large international following and have been praised for their representation of girl power and confidence in their music and performances.
