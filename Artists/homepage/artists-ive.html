About IVE


IVE is a South Korean girl group formed in 2021 under the management of Kakao M. The group consists of five members: Yujin, Hyemee, Suri, Lua, and Nara. Before their official debut, IVE made their pre-debut appearance on Mnet's reality show "Girls Planet 999," where they showcased their talents to a global audience. Their debut single "Eleven" was released on May 3, 2021, and features a combination of pop, R&B, and hip-hop sounds.

Despite being a new group, IVE has already garnered a lot of attention from fans and music critics alike. Their powerful vocals, intricate choreography, and unique concept have all contributed to their rising popularity. The group's name is said to represent the idea of "I've," which represents self-affirmation and the power of positive thinking. With their dynamic energy and fresh approach to music, IVE is poised to make a big impact on the K-pop scene and beyond.
