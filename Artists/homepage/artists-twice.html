About TWICE


TWICE is a South Korean girl group that debuted in 2015 under JYP Entertainment. The group consists of nine members: Nayeon, Jeongyeon, Momo, Sana, Jihyo, Mina, Dahyun, Chaeyoung, and Tzuyu. They are known for their catchy songs, adorable personalities, and energetic dance performances. TWICE rose to fame quickly after their debut, with their first mini-album "The Story Begins" and its lead single "Like OOH-AHH" becoming a commercial success. Since then, they have released numerous chart-topping hits such as "Cheer Up," "TT," "Likey," and "Fancy." TWICE has also gained popularity internationally, with successful tours in Asia, the United States, and Europe. In addition to their music, the group is known for their charming personalities and frequent appearances on variety shows.
