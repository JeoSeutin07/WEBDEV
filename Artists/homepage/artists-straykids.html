About Straykids


Stray Kids is a South Korean boy group formed by JYP Entertainment in 2018. The group consists of eight members: Bang Chan, Lee Know, Changbin, Hyunjin, Han, Felix, Seungmin, and I.N. The group was formed through a reality show called "Stray Kids," where the members showcased their skills and talent. The group debuted on March 25, 2018, with their album "I Am Not," which includes the title track "District 9."

Stray Kids' music style is a mix of hip hop, electronic, and pop, with lyrics often written by the members themselves. They are known for their powerful and energetic performances, showcasing their sharp choreography and dynamic vocals. Stray Kids has gained a large international following, particularly among younger audiences, for their relatable lyrics and messages of self-discovery and empowerment.
