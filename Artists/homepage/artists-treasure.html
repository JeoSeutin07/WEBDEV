About Treasure


Treasure is a South Korean boy group formed by YG Entertainment in 2019. The group consists of 12 members: Hyunsuk, Jihoon, Yoshi, Junkyu, Mashiho, Jaehyuk, Asahi, Yedam, Doyoung, Haruto, Jeongwoo, and Junghwan. They debuted on August 7, 2020, with their single album "The First Step: Chapter One" and have since released several successful albums and singles.

Before their official debut, Treasure participated in the survival show "YG Treasure Box" in 2019, where they competed against other trainees from the company to secure a spot in the final lineup. Throughout the show, the members showcased their talent and charisma, winning over fans with their distinct personalities and impressive performances. Treasure has since become known for their energetic and dynamic performances, catchy songs, and unique visuals, making them a rising force in the K-pop industry.
