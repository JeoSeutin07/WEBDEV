About EXO


EXO is a popular K-pop group that debuted in 2012 under SM Entertainment. The group originally consisted of twelve members, divided into two sub-units: EXO-K and EXO-M. EXO-K focuses on performing in Korean, while EXO-M performs in Mandarin for the Chinese market. In 2014, three members left the group, leaving nine members to continue their activities. The group has achieved significant success both domestically and internationally, winning multiple awards and breaking various records.
