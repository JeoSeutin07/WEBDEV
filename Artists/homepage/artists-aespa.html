About aespa


Aespa is a South Korean girl group formed by SM Entertainment in 2020. The group consists of four members: Karina, Giselle, Winter, and Ningning. Their name is a combination of "Ã¦", which represents "Avatar X Experience", and "aspect", meaning various sides or perspectives. Aespa debuted with their single "Black Mamba" on November 17, 2020.

Karina, the leader and main dancer of the group, was the first member to be introduced. She trained for three years and seven months before debuting with Aespa. Giselle, the main rapper and vocalist, is from Japan and was scouted through SM's global audition in 2018. Winter, the main vocalist, was born in 2001 and trained for two years before joining Aespa. Ningning, the lead vocalist, was born in China and was also scouted through SM's global audition in 2016.

Aespa has been praised for their unique concept and high-quality music videos. They have also been recognized for their strong vocals and impressive dance performances. Despite being a new group, Aespa has already gained a large following and has become one of the most popular K-pop girl groups in the industry.
