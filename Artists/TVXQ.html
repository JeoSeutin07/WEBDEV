TVXQ (동방신기)

TVXQ (동방신기) currently consists of 2 members: Yunho and Changmin. TVXQ debuted as a 5 members group on December 26, 2003, under SM Entertainment with the single “Hug“. In July 2009, members Jaejoong, Yoochun, and Junsu filled a lawsuit to split with SM Entertainment, claiming unfair treatment. The members’ last public performance as 5 members was on December 31, 2009 when they performed “Stand by U“
